What Is This

The Sacred Vault

The Treasury is a Solana wallet controlled by the $KEK team. It exists for one purpose: to fund the things that make $KEK grow. There are no hidden wallets, no VC allocations, no team tokens. The address is published here so anyone can verify on-chain what comes in and what goes out.

Contributions are entirely voluntary. If you believe in the project and want to help fund its next move — marketing, liquidity, listings, development — this is where that happens.

Deflationary Mechanics

The Burn Altar

Burning $KEK means sending tokens to a permanently inaccessible address — they are gone from the supply forever. A smaller supply means each remaining token represents a larger share of the total. The Temple records all burns in the Canon.

During Great Burn rituals, the community coordinates mass burns. The altar below tracks the cumulative total.
